Operating since 1995, Friend In Need is a non-profit organization focused on provding rural communities the health care, education and support they need.

Location: Sholurmattam, Kil Kotagiri surroundings
Year Started: 1995
Tribe Count: 5
Population: 1L+

Mission

Free Medical Aid

Providing free medical aid serves the community by offering quality healthcare services to those in need, regardless of their ability to pay. We conduct health checkup camps to aid eye, dental and general health checkup service.

Community Health Education

Focused on community health awareness programs by raising awareness on important health topics and provides education to improve the overall health and wellness of the community. The goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge and resources needed to maintain a healthy lifestyle

Environmental Awarness Program

Focused on community environmental awareness programs by striving to educate the public on the importance of preserving and protecting the environment. The goal is to promote sustainable practices and encourage community members to take an active role in preserving natural resources for future generations.

Tackling Social Economic Problems

Strive to improve the living standards and promote economic development by providing access to education, healthcare, and job training opportunities. We work to empower the tribal community and break the cycle of poverty.
